Curriculum Vitae

Steven Ullman is a fifth-year Ph.D. student in the Management Information Systems (MIS) department at the University of Arizona. He is a research associate in the Artificial Intelligence (AI) Lab under the guidance of Dr. Hsinchun Chen. Prior to this, Steven earned a BS in Computer Information Systems (CIS) and an MBA from Colorado State University Pueblo (2013-2018), as well as an MS in MIS from UArizona (2018-2019).
